    Lotus's in Malaysia

    At Lotus's, we're here to create a more delightful shopping experience for our customers by helping them make smarter everyday choices.


    We know that every customer' needs are unique, with a desire for personalisation. Our aspiration is to be a smart retailer, a one-stop service, offline and online where customers can enjoy a fast and seamless shopping experience anytime and anywhere, while continuing to offer quality fresh food, better products at affordable prices.


    As one of Malaysia's leading hypermarket operators, we now employ around 9,000 people Peninsular Malaysia, operate 64 stores and two distribution centres, and was the first grocery retailer in the country to introduce its own online grocery shopping platform. Today, our services are also available via the Lotus's Shop Online, Order & Collect as well as on Shopee, Lazada, Foodpanda and Grab Mart.
